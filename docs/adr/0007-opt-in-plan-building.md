# Opt-in Plan building: users add Sales explicitly rather than dropping unwanted ones

A weekly Scan within a 30-mile radius produces 20–30 Hunt-matching Sales; a typical day visits 3–5. Opt-out (drop the 20+ you're skipping) is tedious at that scale. Opt-in (tap `+ Plan` on the cards you want) is faster and more intentional. The Plan section appears on first add and remains empty by default, making it clear no commitment has been made until the user acts.
